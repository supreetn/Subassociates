    Launched in January 2003, SUB Associates provides Global Management, Corporate Training, and Executive Staffing services to the Nanotechnology, Information Technology, Medical Sciences, Clean Technology, and Telecommunication industry. SUB Associates offers well-focused services to our valuable clients, regardless of the location or size of the company.

  • Q. How do we begin working with Sub Associates?

    Once a need for our services is identified, we submit a proposal, outlining a clear set of deliverables and an estimated cost for our services. Upon mutual agreement, we will begin the project and review progress on a regular basis. Once the project has been completed, a closure meeting is set up to make sure that all expectations were met and that there are no loose ends. On-going "service" support can be provided to our clients as needed.
