About Us

Welcome To Sub Associates, LLC

Bridging Gaps with Passion since 2003.

SUB Associates® offers core competencies needed to aspire and achieve organizational and individual’s goals. We inspire enterprising and creative people with unparalleled opportunities for professional and personal growth to our clients. Our aim is to help our clients make decisive improvements in their direction and performance by sparking breakthrough ideas and garnering crucial resources for clients, the business world, and society at large. We work closely with clients to convert insights into strategies to achieve a substantial positive impact on performance.

In addition; SUB Associates® assists clients for strategic, operational, corporate training and executive staffing needs and to explore extraordinary opportunities, manage and sustain growth, minimize cost and maximize revenue. We utilize unique and proven methodologies to deliver actionable results to our clients in an efficient, timely and cost-effective manner. Our expertise are in the field of: Semiconductor, Pharmaceuticals, Bio-med, Information Technology, Network Security, Compound Semiconductor, Equipment, Gases, Chemicals, Healthcare, Academia, Communication, Lasers, Optical, Solar, Clean Technology, Nanotechnology, CRM, SCM, QSS, Employment Techniques and e-Management.
